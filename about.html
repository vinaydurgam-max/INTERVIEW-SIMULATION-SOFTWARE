About This Site

The Interview Simulation System is designed to facilitate a structured and unbiased interview process. The system helps both interviewers and candidates prepare for and engage in interviews that are relevant to the candidate's area of expertise.

The system provides a virtual boardroom experience where interviewers can ask relevant questions, and candidates can respond in real-time. The goal is to ensure that the questions asked and the answers given are both meaningful and reflective of the candidate's knowledge and skills.
